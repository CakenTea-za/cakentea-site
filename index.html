Get to know us!

Founded in 2021, CakenTea was born out of a passion for storytelling and a deep-rooted commitment to quality. With a strong background in video animation, graphic design, and videography, we’ve honed our craft in the vibrant, creative landscape of Johannesburg, South Africa—where inspiration pours out of every corner.

Since our inception, we’ve had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients, including non-profit organisations and government sectors, helping them bring their visions to life through compelling multimedia content.

At CakenTea, we’re more than just a production agency—we’re a partner in your creative journey, dedicated to making your story shine.
